Roof

I purchased this house in 2021, fully aware that the roof above the front door was likely leaking. A few months later, I took on the task of re-shingling the roof. It was a lot of work, but the outcome was definitely worth it.

❮

❯

Kitchen

Another significant upgrade I made to the house was a complete kitchen renovation. It was clear the previous owners just slapped on some paint and called it a day. Some of the more notable improvements I made include tiling the floor, making custom cabinets, and removing a window.

❮

❯

Linen Closet

The previous owners seemed to have renovated the bathroom, but they left the original shower in place. I replaced it with a linen closet, simply adding shelves in its place.

❮

❯

Misc

I've done lots of smaller projects that don't necessarily warrant their own dedicated posts including things like painting the rooms, building saw accessories, and adding stepping stones out the back of the house.

❮

❯

Entryway

I wasn't a huge fan of the massive entryway closet. I decided to open it up a little and add a hall tree in place of the closet.

❮

❯

Siding

I knew replacing the shake style siding was something I wanted to do, but it took a lot longer to get to this point than anticipated. A lot of work went into this as it also involved replacing the soffit, gutters, and fascia trim.
