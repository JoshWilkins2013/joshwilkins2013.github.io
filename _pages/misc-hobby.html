V-Arm

A partial body immersion device for VR that enables users to physically engage with virtual objects. It provides kinesthetic feedback by adjusting the user's arm in response to interactions with virtual elements. This is accomplished through the use of air muscles; inflatable bladders with mesh wrapping that can contract using pressurized air.

Wilmake

Back when 3D printers were just coming out, I wanted to make one that would be more useful. I attempted to create an interchangeable head so that it could also draw, laser engrave, mill, and scan 3D objects. Not long after working on this project, this kickstarter came along with a very similar concept, making just over $1.6 million!

Ambilight

An ambient lighting system that creates background lighting based on TV content. It is capable of producing different shades, brightness, and intensities of color based upon frame content. I used Rasplex to stream video from a raspberry pi and Hyperion to implement the lighting backdrop.
