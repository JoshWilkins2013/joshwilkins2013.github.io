{% assign images_dir = "img/Analytics/Car_Analysis" %} {% capture block_description %} An alternative benefit of my price abstractor tool is having access to real-world car and rental data! From this data, I have analyzed which car model was best within each make and which model was best overall. The results are intuitive; the more you spend on a car, the faster it will depreciate! Perhaps if the prices were normalized by their initial price, a more even distribution of depreciation rates would be seen, indicating all cars depreciate at the same weighted rate.
